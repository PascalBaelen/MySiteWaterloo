"A ses compagnons de gloire, sa dernière pensée, Sainte Helene 5 mai 1815"










Vive l'Empereur !

My name is Baelen Pascal... I was born on 20 December 1797

Forgot to tell, I was born in Paris, France, where Napoleon went from General Bonaparte to Premier Consul, and finally crown himself to Emperor Napoleon.

What's my story ? Well I was unlucky and got drafted at the age of 18, and had to fight for the Emperor during the 100 days as a conscript.

Together with the other "greycoats" we walked from Paris to Belgium, where we fought three days, starting the 16th June 1815 at Quatre-bras...

Then the 17th at Ligny, and ending, at Waterloo the 18th June 1815, where my Emperor fought his last battle !

Waterloo ! Waterloo ! Waterloo ! morne plaine !
