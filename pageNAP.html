Napoleonic Warfare reenactors

Glad you made it ! Well, I'm not insane, I just like the Napoleonic period and have done some reenactment in the past !

I also like to collect stuff from this period, documents, battlefield relics, engravings, sabres, old books (memoires f.i. Les mémoires du général Marbot), old pocket watches etc ...

And of course, it's a good thing living only 35 km away from Waterloo ...

I must admit however, that sometimes, and for a brief moment, I may lose myself back to the past, seeing everybody around me, living and dressed like 200 years ago ...

I could go on for centuries about this topic, but this humble site is just to wet your appetites...

Meanwhile, my friends and family know me as "Le soldat de Waterloo, Jean-Baptiste Baelen", in honor of my ancestor, born in 1797, died in 1815 at Mont-Saint-Jean (Waterloo)
